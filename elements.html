My weaknesses with 3D mapping

It could be a matter of being a two dimensional old school kind of mapper but I must confess, 3D is just my achilles heel.The brilliant minds out ther have made it as easy as 1 - 2 - click in GIS BLENDER but I just cannot stand making 3D maps. Do not stone me just yet, the things ray shader can do, Wow!! I respect the mother nature out of it but for now I say flat plane 2D's are my jam. Maybe 2023 will be different.

When one map leads to another

The day I made this map was the day I was asked to be the God mother to my niece. On finishing it, I was not satisfied with the message that the data was showing me as a static map. So my plan is to have it as a two window interactive map in which as a user pans over a region and the population data is displayed so as to allow for ease in comparison.
